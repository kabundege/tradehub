Who we are & History

Founded in 2023, Trade Hub Ltd is a dynamic company based in Rwanda, specializing in the export of high-quality horticultural products, with a focus on has avocados. As a company with a small but dedicated team, we are committed to establishing Rwanda as a key player in the global horticultural market.

Vision

Looking forward, our goal is to expand our reach, connecting Rwanda's rich agricultural products with markets across the globe. We are actively seeking partnerships and opportunities to showcase the best of what Rwanda has to offer.

Mission

Our mission is to forge reliable and sustainable trade partnerships worldwide. We strive to deliver exceptional quality and service, ensuring that our products meet the highest international standards.
